Services

Web Development

I can design and develop a website that suits your specific business needs. Whether that is a website to show off your small business or a web application that can track your employee’s hours. Just tell me what problem you need solved and we can come up with a solution together.

Websites

Your website is a representation of your business and it’s crucial that it’s done properly. I ensure all websites I make are well designed, intuitive to use, available in all the popular browsers, responsive to fit devices of all sizes, and optimized for the popular search engines (SEO). If you’re interested, I can include analytics to get insights about the visitors and interactions on your website.

When creating your website, I don’t just reuse templates. I take your needs and the message that you want to communicate to create a simple, yet unique website that will accurately represent your business.

Web Applications

Do you need something a little more complex than a website? I can develop a web application. A few examples of web applications I’ve built include a time and expense tracking application for employees, a billing application for a law firm, a store for purchasing courses online, a portal for displaying data received from various sensors, and a dashboard that lists current items needing to be assembled.

Are you interested in working with me to build you a website or a web application? Then get in touch with the form below or you can email me.
